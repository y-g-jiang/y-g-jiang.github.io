                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              1. What This Project Is

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              1. 这个项目是什么

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Jiangtherapee is not a single-purpose viewer; it combines RAW decoding, display rendering, ROI measurement, spectral analysis, SFR analysis, color analysis, burst stacking, DNG export, and a lightweight Photoshop-like local adjustment system.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Jiangtherapee 不是单一用途的看图器；它把 RAW 解码、显示渲染、ROI 测量、频谱分析、SFR 分析、色彩分析、连拍堆栈、DNG 导出以及一个轻量级类 Photoshop 局部调整系统整合在了一起。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The codebase mixes production-style UI with many research tools, so some panels feel like lab instruments rather than consumer product dialogs.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 这套代码同时混合了产品化界面和大量研究型工具，因此有些面板更像实验仪器而不是面向普通用户的消费级弹窗。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The main entry point is index.tsx, which mounts App.tsx; almost every user-facing workflow eventually flows through App.tsx.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 主入口是 index.tsx，它挂载 App.tsx；几乎所有用户可见流程最后都会经过 App.tsx。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              49. 单个 ROI 如何变成 Edge Data

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • handleAddROI() in SfrModal.tsx is the main manual-ingestion point: it checks the current source family, crops the appropriate data, and then funnels that crop into calculateEdgeData(...) or calculateEdgeDataWithManualLine(...).
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • SfrModal.tsx 里的 handleAddROI() 是主要的手动接收入口：它会先判断当前输入源类型，裁出对应数据，然后统一把裁剪结果送进 calculateEdgeData(...) 或 calculateEdgeDataWithManualLine(...)。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • If the user enabled Pick Edge Line, geometry comes from the two selected canvas points; otherwise the processor estimates the edge from image content.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 如果用户启用了 Pick Edge Line，几何信息就来自画布上手动点出的两点；否则处理器会从图像内容里自行估计边缘。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • For three-plane input, the processor either picks one RGB channel or computes luma from 0.2126 R + 0.7152 G + 0.0722 B.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 对于三平面输入，处理器要么直接选一个 RGB 通道，要么用 0.2126 R + 0.7152 G + 0.0722 B 计算亮度。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • For scalar Bayer input in green-only work, the processor builds two related datasets: an interpolated green guide plane used for locating the edge, and a separate set of real green samples used later for projection and ESF/LSF.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 对于 Bayer 绿通道测量，处理器会构建两套相关数据：一套是用于定位边缘的插值绿引导平面，另一套是后续用于投影和 ESF/LSF 的真实绿采样点。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Auto Block measurements also end up as EdgeData; the difference is only that those ROIs come from the detection pipeline rather than from manually drawn rectangles.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Auto Block 里的自动测量最终也会落成 EdgeData；区别只在于这些 ROI 来自检测流程，而不是手动画出的矩形。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              50. Edge Orientation And Edge-Line Fitting

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              50. 边缘方向判断与边缘直线拟合

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • calculateEdgeData() first estimates whether the edge is more horizontal or more vertical by comparing accumulated gradients, and then normalizes the ROI into a common working orientation.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • calculateEdgeData() 会先通过累计梯度判断边缘更偏水平还是更偏垂直，然后把 ROI 归一到统一的工作朝向。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • In the generic manual path, orientation normalization is followed by the familiar row-wise or column-wise local-derivative centroid collection and straight-line regression.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 在通用手动路径里，方向归一之后仍然会沿用熟悉的逐行或逐列局部导数质心收集，再做直线回归。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • In the Bayer-green path, edge location is guided by the interpolated green image, but final projection still uses only the true green phase samples.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 在 Bayer 绿通道路径中，边缘位置由插值绿图引导，但最终投影仍然只使用真实绿相位采样。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • In the newer gray / BW / three-plane auto-refine path, the code refines each side by sweeping scan lines along the edge tangent, finding derivative centroids along the normal, and then fitting the final line only from those centroid points.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 在较新的灰度 / BW / 三平面自动精修路径中，代码会沿边的切向扫出一组扫描线，在法向上寻找导数质心点，然后只用这些质心点来拟合最终直线。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • That change is important because it is more robust than letting a loose cloud of edge pixels directly dominate the final line, especially in three-plane work.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 这个变化很重要，因为它比直接让一团松散边缘像素主导最终直线更稳健，尤其是在三平面测量里更明显。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • If too few valid rows, scans, or centroids survive, the ROI is rejected as not containing a reliable measurable edge.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 如果保留下来的有效行、扫描线或质心点太少，ROI 就会被判定为不含可靠可测边缘而被拒绝。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              51. Distance Projection, Binning, And ESF

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              51. 距离投影、分箱与 ESF

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Once the edge line is known, each accepted sample is projected onto the edge-normal direction, producing a signed distance from the edge.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 一旦边缘直线确定下来，每个被接受的样本都会被投影到边缘法线方向，从而得到一个相对边缘的有符号距离。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • These distances are sorted and binned into an oversampled edge spread function, or ESF.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 这些距离会被排序并分箱，形成一个过采样的边缘扩展函数，也就是 ESF。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • In the expert manual panel, Bin still supports Manual entry; when enabled, the value is clamped into [0.01, 0.5].
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 在专家手动面板里，Bin 仍然支持 Manual 输入；启用后，该数值会被夹到 [0.01, 0.5] 区间内。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Auto mode no longer exposes a manual bin box in the left panel. Instead, each detected edge chooses its own best bin automatically.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • auto 模式在左侧面板里已经不再暴露手动 bin 输入框；取而代之的是每条已检测边都自动选择自己的最优 bin。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The current auto rule is: choose the finest bin that produces no empty bins inside the central range center ± 0.25 * ROI short-side pixels, but never go finer than 0.125.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 当前 auto 规则是：在 中心 ± 0.25 * ROI 短边像素数 的范围内，选择“不会产生空箱”的最细 bin，但不会细于 0.125。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Empty bins are still back-filled using the previous valid value so ESF remains differentiable enough for later steps; there is no extra instability-smoothing layer beyond that.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 空 bin 仍然会用前一个有效值回填，以保证 ESF 在后续步骤中仍足够可求导；除此之外不会额外套一层“不稳定 bin 平滑”。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • If the early bins are brighter than the late bins, ESF polarity is flipped so edge direction remains consistent.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 如果前面的 bin 比后面的 bin 更亮，函数就会翻转 ESF 极性，以保证边缘方向保持一致。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              52. From ESF To LSF

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              52. 从 ESF 到 LSF

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • After ESF is built, the code computes the line spread function lsfFull using a centered finite difference.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • ESF 构建完成后，代码会用中心差分来计算线扩展函数 lsfFull。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • This derivative step stays intentionally simple and stable; more advanced compensation happens later during SFR computation rather than here.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 这一步导数实现刻意保持简单稳定；更复杂的补偿不在这里做，而是在后面的 SFR 计算阶段完成。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The returned EdgeData object contains the ESF/LSF pair, bin size, orientation, and fitted edge geometry, which is enough for later averaging and frequency-domain analysis.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 返回的 EdgeData 对象包含 ESF/LSF、bin size、方向以及拟合好的边缘几何，这已经足够供后续做平均与频域分析。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              53. How computeSFR() Works

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              53. computeSFR() 的具体工作方式

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • computeSFR() accepts one or more EdgeData samples, so the modal can accumulate multiple ROIs and average them into one result.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • computeSFR() 接受一个或多个 EdgeData 样本，因此这个面板可以累积多个 ROI 并把它们平均成一个结果。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The function uses a fixed FFT size of 4096 and a common output frequency grid with 501 points.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 这个函数固定使用大小为 4096 的 FFT，并建立一个含 501 个点的统一输出频率轴。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • If pixel pitch is available, frequencies are scaled into lp/mm; otherwise they remain in cy/px.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 如果像元尺寸可用，频率会被换算成 lp/mm；否则就保持在 cy/px。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The code always computes out to 2x Nyquist internally, while the UI can later choose whether to show the normal range or the extended range.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 代码在内部总是把频率算到 2x Nyquist，而界面层之后再决定是只显示常规范围还是扩展范围。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Auto-overlay MTF50 labels, batch SFR results, the embedded expert plots, and the point-set / export modal all ultimately rely on computeSFR().
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 自动叠加层上的 MTF50 标签、批量 SFR 结果、嵌入式专家图表以及点集 / 导出弹窗，本质上最终都依赖 computeSFR()。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The Line Pair view in the plot modal is not a separate measurement pipeline; it samples the current MTF curve at fixed spatial frequencies such as 10 / 20 / 40 / 80 lp/mm once pixel size is known.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 绘图弹窗里的 Line Pair 视图并不是一条独立测量流水线；它是在已知像元尺寸之后，从当前 MTF 曲线上读取 10 / 20 / 40 / 80 lp/mm 等固定空间频率处的数值。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              54. Peak Detection, Deshading, And Radius Selection

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              54. 峰值检测、去渐晕与半径选择

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • For each edge, computeSFR() locates the LSF peak and estimates a width using the points where LSF falls below 20% of that peak.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 对每条边，computeSFR() 会先找到 LSF 峰值，然后通过 LSF 下降到峰值 20% 以下的位置估计一个宽度。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • If Deshading is enabled, the function performs a linear detrend on the LSF tails outside an exclusion band around the peak.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 如果启用了 Deshading，函数就会在峰值附近排除一段区域，然后对两侧尾部做线性去趋势。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • If Limit Fit is enabled in the UI, the tail detrend uses only a bounded fitting width; otherwise it uses all eligible tail samples.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 如果在界面里启用了 Limit Fit，尾部去趋势就只会使用有限宽度的拟合区；否则会使用所有符合条件的尾部样本。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The embedded expert core still exposes Jiang's official NR, which is the current auto-radius mode. Turning it off switches to manual radius control.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 嵌入式专家核心里仍然保留 Jiang's official NR，它就是当前的自动半径模式；关闭后则切换到手动半径控制。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • In contrast, the left SFR workflow panel no longer exposes a separate Official NR toggle, and the normal batch-workflow path treats NR as off.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 与此相对，左侧 SFR 工作流面板已经不再提供单独的 Official NR 开关，常规批处理流程会把 NR 按关闭处理。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Deshading is now shared between the left workflow panel and the embedded core. Toggling it also refreshes auto-measurement labels, explain runs, and the point-set / plot export data.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Deshading 现在在左侧工作流面板和嵌入式核心之间共享；切换它时，也会同步刷新自动测量标签、explain 结果以及点集 / 绘图导出数据。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Candidate purity, coverage, and similar auto-detection thresholds are screening logic for whether a block should enter measurement; they are not the main MTF computation itself.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 候选纯净度、覆盖率之类的 auto 检测阈值，本质上属于“这个块应不应该进入测量”的筛选逻辑，而不是主 MTF 计算本体。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              55. FFT Preparation, Hann Window, And MTF/PTF

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              55. FFT 准备、Hann 窗以及 MTF/PTF

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The cropped LSF is not sent directly into FFT in-place; the code first circularly shifts the local LSF peak to index 0.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 被裁剪后的 LSF 并不会原样送进 FFT；代码会先把局部峰值循环平移到索引 0。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • This circular shift is an important implementation detail because it removes a large linear phase ramp and keeps the phase response interpretable.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 这个循环平移是一个很关键的实现细节，因为它能消除很大的线性相位斜坡，让相位响应更可解释。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • If Hann On is enabled, a Hann window is multiplied into the cropped LSF before the FFT.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 如果启用了 Hann On，则会在 FFT 之前先把 Hann 窗乘到裁剪后的 LSF 上。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The FFT magnitude becomes the raw MTF, and the complex angle becomes the raw phase used later for PTF.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • FFT 的幅值会变成原始 MTF，而复数相角则会成为后续 PTF 的原始相位来源。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • MTF is normalized by the DC component and then interpolated onto the common frequency grid so multiple ROIs can be accumulated consistently.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • MTF 会先用直流分量做归一化，然后插值到统一频率轴上，这样多个 ROI 才能被一致地叠加平均。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • PTF is taken only from the first valid ROI and then processed by phase unwrapping plus linear detrending before interpolation to the same frequency axis.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • PTF 只从第一个有效 ROI 里提取，然后先做相位展开，再做线性去趋势，最后插值到同一条频率轴上。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • In the current UI, however, the PTF toggle is intentionally disabled because that presentation path is considered not final yet even though the lower-level computation still exists.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 不过在当前界面里，PTF 开关被有意设为不可用，因为这条展示路径目前被视为尚未定稿，尽管底层计算仍然存在。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              56. How MTF50 Is Computed

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              56. MTF50 是怎么求出来的

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • After averaging all normalized ROI MTF curves, computeSFR() searches for the first crossing where the MTF falls from above 0.5 to below 0.5.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 在把所有归一化 ROI 的 MTF 曲线平均之后，computeSFR() 会寻找第一处从高于 0.5 掉到低于 0.5 的交点。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The exact MTF50 value is then linearly interpolated between those two neighboring samples.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 然后再在这两个相邻采样点之间做线性插值，得到精确的 MTF50。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • This means the displayed MTF50 is not just the nearest sampled bin, but a continuous estimate derived from the sampled curve.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 这意味着界面上显示的 MTF50 不是最接近的离散 bin，而是从采样曲线插值得到的连续估计值。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              57. What The SFR UI Actually Exposes

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              57. SFR 界面实际暴露了什么

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Pixel Pitch changes the x-axis unit from cy/px to lp/mm, and Image Height enables an additional LW/PH style derivation from the measured MTF50.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Pixel Pitch 会把横轴单位从 cy/px 转成 lp/mm，而 Image Height 则允许在 MTF50 基础上进一步推导出 LW/PH 风格指标。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The Bin box shows the current oversampling step and warns when the automatic bin reaches its upper limit, which the UI interprets as a hint that the ROI may be too small.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Bin 区块会显示当前的过采样步长；如果自动 bin 已经顶到上限，界面会把它解释为“ROI 可能过小”的提示。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • G-Only is shown only for Bayer-like scalar data; in three-plane mode that control is replaced by explicit source selection.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • G-Only 只会在 Bayer 风格的标量数据上出现；一旦进入三平面模式，这个开关就会被明确的数据源选择控件替代。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Add Current ROI appends another edge sample to the current SFR stack instead of replacing the existing result.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Add Current ROI 不是覆盖当前结果，而是把新边缘样本继续追加到当前 SFR 样本堆里。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Export CSV writes Frequency, MTF, Phase columns, while Copy Pos/MTF50 copies the ROI center and current MTF50 in a compact tuple form.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Export CSV 会写出 Frequency, MTF, Phase 三列，而 Copy Pos/MTF50 会把 ROI 中心和当前 MTF50 以紧凑 tuple 形式复制出去。

Built-In Optical Model And Auto Fit

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              58. 内建光学模型与自动拟合

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • SfrModal.tsx contains a forward optical MTF model with parameters such as aperture, olpfSeparationPx, fillFactor, coreSigmaPx, haloSigmaPx, haloWeight, wavelengthUm, and rolloff.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • SfrModal.tsx 内置了一套正向光学 MTF 模型，参数包括 aperture、olpfSeparationPx、fillFactor、coreSigmaPx、haloSigmaPx、haloWeight、wavelengthUm 和 rolloff。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The UI lets the user lock any subset of these parameters so fitting can optimize only the unlocked subset.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 界面允许用户锁定任意子集参数，使拟合过程只优化未锁定的那部分。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The fit objective is a weighted squared error between measured and modeled MTF, with a small regularization penalty on some parameters.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 拟合目标函数是测量 MTF 和模型 MTF 之间的加权平方误差，并对某些参数附加轻微正则化惩罚。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The optimizer is a custom Nelder-Mead implementation with 7 restarts and 320 iterations per restart, seeded by the current UI parameter values plus random jitter for restart diversity.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 优化器是自定义的 Nelder-Mead 实现，使用 7 次重启、每次 320 轮迭代；其初值来自当前界面参数，并在重启时加入随机抖动以提升搜索多样性。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The fit range is user-bounded by fitMinFreq and fitMaxFreq, and the modal refuses fitting if fewer than 10 MTF samples remain inside the chosen interval.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 拟合频段由 fitMinFreq 和 fitMaxFreq 约束；如果所选区间里剩下的 MTF 采样点少于 10 个，弹窗会拒绝执行拟合。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              59. Auto SFR Detection Pipeline

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              59. 自动 SFR 检测流水线

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • autoDetectAndComputeSFR() in sfrProcessor.ts is the batch-style auto-detection path.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • sfrProcessor.ts 里的 autoDetectAndComputeSFR() 就是批量风格的自动检测路径。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • It scans the rendered image in 10x10 blocks, computes a Sobel-like local gradient from the RGBA preview, and keeps only blocks whose gradient magnitude and angle fall inside user-provided thresholds.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 它会以 10x10 的 block 扫描渲染后的图像，用类似 Sobel 的局部梯度从 RGBA 预览中求边缘，并只保留那些梯度强度和角度落在用户阈值范围内的 block。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Edge orientation is normalized into a 0 to 90 effective-angle measure so that near-horizontal and near-vertical edge families can be filtered symmetrically.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 边缘方向会被归一到 0 到 90 的有效角度空间里，这样接近水平和接近垂直的边缘就能被对称地筛选。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Neighboring accepted blocks are merged with a BFS-style grouping step, but only when their angles are close enough, specifically with a difference under 15 degrees or the wraparound equivalent.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 相邻的合格 block 会通过 BFS 风格的聚类步骤合并，但前提是它们的角度足够接近，具体条件是角差小于 15 度或满足绕回等价条件。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Each merged group is converted into a candidate ROI whose aspect ratio depends on whether the average edge is more vertical-like or horizontal-like.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 每个聚合后的 group 都会被转换成一个候选 ROI，而这个 ROI 的宽高比例会根据平均边缘更偏垂直还是更偏水平来决定。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The function then reruns the normal calculateEdgeData() plus computeSFR() pipeline on each candidate ROI and returns only those groups that successfully produce a numeric mtf50.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 接着，函数会对每个候选 ROI 再次运行标准的 calculateEdgeData() 加 computeSFR() 流程，并只保留那些成功得到数值型 mtf50 的 group。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              60. Practical Reading Of The SFR Design

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              60. 对这套 SFR 设计的实际理解

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The SFR Lab is therefore not a single “measure sharpness” function; it is a layered system consisting of source normalization, edge extraction, oversampled ESF construction, derivative-based LSF generation, optional detrending, FFT-based MTF/PTF generation, multi-ROI averaging, and optional forward-model fitting.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 因此 SFR Lab 并不是一个单独的“测锐度”函数；它实际上是一个分层系统，由输入源归一化、边缘提取、过采样 ESF 构建、基于导数的 LSF 生成、可选去趋势、基于 FFT 的 MTF/PTF 生成、多 ROI 平均以及可选的正向模型拟合共同组成。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The most important user-facing implication is that ROI quality, source-path choice, and preprocessing switches can change the result at least as much as the final FFT step.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 对用户而言最重要的一点是，ROI 质量、输入路径选择和预处理开关，对结果的影响至少和最后的 FFT 步骤一样大。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The most important developer-facing implication is that almost every SFR number shown in the UI is the end product of several explicit geometric and signal-processing decisions that are all inspectable in code.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 对开发者而言最重要的一点是，界面里显示的几乎每一个 SFR 数字，都是多个明确的几何与信号处理决策共同作用后的结果，而且这些决策都可以在代码里逐项追踪。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 为了防止图表 DOM 过载，显示层会做强力降采样，但拟合层使用的是更深、更偏信号意义的数据集，而不是只用界面上的那几十个点。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • dominantChannel 这个概念贯穿整个拟合与派生统计流程，因为后续很多值，比如增益和动态范围，都是相对于被认为最有代表性或最先饱和的那个通道来定义的。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 如果主导通道的可用点太少，拟合会退回更宽泛的 baseData，这让系统在某个通道数据过稀时仍能工作。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              63. PTC Noise Model Fitting

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              63. PTC 噪声模型拟合

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The fitting model is a constrained quadratic variance model of the form variance = a + b * signal + c * signal^2.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 这套拟合模型本质上是一个受约束的二次方差模型，形式是 variance = a + b * signal + c * signal^2。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • In that interpretation, a maps to read-noise variance, b maps inversely to gain, and c maps to PRNU energy.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 在这个解释下，a 对应读出噪声方差，b 与增益成反比，而 c 对应 PRNU 能量。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The implementation first selects points inside a percentage-of-max-signal fit range, converts standard deviation into variance, and then balances the sample set by signal-density weighting.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 实现上会先按“占最大信号百分比”的拟合区间筛点，把标准差转换成方差，再通过信号密度平衡权重来防止某一段信号区间过度主导拟合。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • It then solves a constrained quadratic system, allowing any subset of a, b, and c to be fixed by parameter locks.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 接着它会求解一个受约束的二次系统，并允许通过参数锁把 a、b、c 中的任意子集固定住。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • A robust iterative reweighting loop follows, using normalized residuals, MAD-derived scale estimation, and Huber-like weighting to suppress outliers.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 之后还会进入一个稳健迭代重加权循环，利用归一化残差、基于 MAD 的尺度估计，以及类似 Huber 的权重来压制离群点。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The gain-density side panel is not decorative; it is built from effective per-point gain samples derived after subtracting read-noise and PRNU variance components.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 增益密度侧图并不是装饰，它是从逐点有效增益样本构建出来的，而这些样本是在扣除读噪和 PRNU 方差项之后得到的。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 拟合区间以“当前最大信号的百分比”表示，可以通过数值输入、点击选择模式，或直接拖拽图表手柄来修改。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Internally, chart dragging is converted back into signal-space and then back into fit-range percentages, so the UI is geometric but the model remains signal-relative.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 在内部，图表拖拽会先被还原到信号空间，再换算回拟合区间百分比，因此界面是几何式的，但模型仍保持相对信号的定义方式。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The modal can overlay theoretical read, shot, PRNU, total, and fit-range guides over the measured point cloud.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 这个弹窗可以把理论读噪、shot、PRNU、总噪声以及拟合区间引导线叠加到实测点云之上。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Export is handled by a custom canvas renderer rather than by screenshotting the DOM, which is why exported JPGs can use special fluorescent scatter rendering and summary annotations.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 导出并不是简单地截图 DOM，而是走自定义 canvas 渲染器，因此导出的 JPG 才能应用特殊的荧光散点样式和摘要注释。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 当前代码还支持一个专门的导出设置面板，用户可以在真正生成 JPG 之前决定是否启用放大的 glow 点样式。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • StackingModal.tsx 是一个面向公式驱动多帧处理引擎的操作台。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 用户并不是简单地加载一个文件夹然后点合并；相反，文件会先映射到诸如 A、B、C 这样的符号标识，再由一条文本公式决定它们如何组合。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 这个弹窗会把公式持久化到 localStorage，校验黑电平，校验对齐参数，记录执行日志，最后再调用 processStacking(...)。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The output mode can be either mosaic-oriented dng16 or RGB-oriented jdngf32, and that choice changes not only file format but also the legal internal data path.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 输出模式可以是面向马赛克的 dng16，也可以是面向 RGB 的 jdngf32；这个选择改变的不只是文件格式，还会改变内部允许的数据路径。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              66. Stacking Formula Engine And Decode Phase

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              66. 堆栈公式引擎与解码阶段

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • processStacking() begins by tokenizing the user formula, converting it to RPN, and collecting the exact image identifiers actually referenced by the expression.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • processStacking() 会先对用户公式做词法切分，再转成逆波兰表达式，并提取出这个表达式真正引用到的图像标识符。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • This means the engine does not blindly decode every loaded file; it decodes only the slots the expression really needs.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 这意味着这个引擎不会盲目解码所有已加载文件，而是只解码表达式真正需要的那些 slot。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Each referenced file is decoded into a RawSlot with dimensions, channel count, CFA pattern, original mask, and float work buffer.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 每个被引用的文件都会被解码成一个 RawSlot，其中包含尺寸、通道数、CFA 模式、原始掩码和浮点工作缓冲区。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • RAW-like files go through processRawDataOnly, while ordinary images can go through processStandardImage, so the stacker can operate on more than one input class.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • RAW 风格文件会走 processRawDataOnly，而普通图像则可以走 processStandardImage，因此这个堆栈器并不只面向单一输入类型。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • A hard dimension-consistency check then guarantees all referenced slots share the same width and height before any alignment or arithmetic happens.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 在任何对齐或算术操作开始之前，系统还会执行一次严格的尺寸一致性检查，确保所有被引用的 slot 具有相同宽高。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              67. Stacking Alignment Modes

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              67. 堆栈对齐模式

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The code explicitly enforces that at most one alignment family can be enabled at a time: align1, align2, or align3.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 代码会显式强制最多只能启用一种对齐家族：align1、align2 或 align3。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • align1 is described as strict HDR+ style block matching.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • align1 被描述为严格的 HDR+ 风格块匹配。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • align2 is the dense-flow style path with a robustness threshold.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • align2 是带有稳健阈值的密集光流风格路径。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • align3 is the most advanced path and can be either ecc or apap, both of which depend on OpenCV.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • align3 是最高级的路径，可以选择 ecc 或 apap，两者都依赖 OpenCV。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • ECC mode builds guide planes, downsamples them to a controlled dimension, optionally normalizes illumination globally, and then estimates a warp with findTransformECC.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • ECC 模式会先构建引导平面，再把它们缩放到受控尺寸，可选地做全局照明归一化，然后通过 findTransformECC 估计变换矩阵。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • APAP mode instead detects features, matches them, solves a locally adaptive warp field, and then remaps the source through those spatially varying maps.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • APAP 模式则会检测特征、进行匹配、求解局部自适应变换场，然后再通过这张空间变化的映射场去重映射源图像。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The alignment subsystem is therefore not a cosmetic pre-pass; it directly determines how per-frame information lands in the later arithmetic evaluation.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 因此对齐子系统绝不是一个装饰性的预处理，它会直接决定每帧信息在后续算术求值中的落位方式。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              68. 堆栈求值与输出封装

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • After alignment, evalRPN(...) computes the user expression in the float domain and returns both the merged data and a source map.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 对齐完成后，evalRPN(...) 会在浮点域里计算用户表达式，并同时返回合并后的数据以及一张 source map。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • An optional legacy-style highlight stretch rescales the entire result so the brightest value fits the output container headroom after output black level is considered.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 还有一个可选的传统式高光拉伸步骤，会在考虑输出黑电平之后，把整个结果重新缩放到适合输出容器头部空间的范围内。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • If the user chose jdngf32, the result is first converted through a drizzle-style RGB kernel approximation, then output black level is added, and finally the data is packed into a custom Float32 JDNG container.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 如果用户选择的是 jdngf32，结果会先经过一种 drizzle 风格的 RGB kernel 近似，再加上输出黑电平，最后被打包进自定义的 Float32 JDNG 容器。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • If the user chose dng16, the result must remain single-channel mosaic data; otherwise the processor throws and asks the user to use Float32 JDNG instead.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 如果用户选择的是 dng16，结果就必须保持单通道马赛克数据；否则处理器会直接报错，并要求用户改用 Float32 JDNG。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Final dng16 packing is done through Pyodide with a Python DNG packer, which mirrors the project’s broader pattern of mixing browser-side JS with targeted Python serialization helpers.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 最终的 dng16 封装是通过 Pyodide 调用 Python DNG 打包器完成的，这延续了整个项目“浏览器端 JS 加定向 Python 序列化辅助”的总体风格。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 普通蒙版本身和图层是分离的，但像 addMaskWithLayer() 和 ensureLayerForMask() 这类辅助函数会把常见的一蒙版一图层模式维持得很顺手。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • paintSelectedMask() 会在局部笔刷范围内直接修改当前选中蒙版的 alpha，而 appendBrushStrokeItemToSelectedMask() 则会把笔刷轨迹记录成可复用的 mask item。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • App.tsx 会为每个 session 维护独立的历史栈，而且每个历史条目都会保存一份深拷贝文档以及对应的变换操作元数据。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • updateCurrentPsDocument(...) 是中心化的文档变更入口；当启用历史追踪时，它会追加新历史条目、执行长度限制，并把历史元数据重新写回当前文档。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 撤销和任意历史跳转会恢复历史快照，同时还会根据预览指纹是否变化来决定清理还是保留临时预览状态。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 预览渲染会先尝试 PS WebGPU，再尝试 PS WebGL，最后在 GPU 不可用或暂时忙碌时回退到 CPU 合成。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 如果只是蒙版局部发生变化且旧预览帧仍存在，composePsDocumentPreviewDirty() 就能只更新脏矩形，而不必整张图完全重算。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • That dirty-rect path is one of the main reasons brush and mask interactions can stay responsive despite the complexity of the document model.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 这个脏区更新路径是笔刷和蒙版交互在文档模型相当复杂时仍能保持响应的重要原因之一。

PS 几何变换流水线

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 文档变换并不只是界面层的观察技巧；它们可以通过裁切、旋转、翻转、拉直、镜头校正和投影变换真正固化进文档。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • App.tsx 里的 materializePsViewDocument() 很清楚地展示了预期顺序：先 upright 变换，再翻转，最后才是视图旋转。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 裁切到 ROI 会基于这个已经物化的视图文档执行，因此裁切的是用户真正看到的画面，而不是某个隐藏的未变换坐标系。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 因此，基于参考线的拉直校正和 Lensfun 畸变校正都被并入同一条更大的变换历史链，而不是孤立存在的显示技巧。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 对于三平面输入，CPU 回退会使用专门的三平面渲染器，而不会强行把它伪装成马赛克数据。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              75. Standard 官方渲染路径

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • renderRawWithOfficial16() uses joraw.js or joraw.wasm through the JoRaw class and asks that runtime to perform a 16-bit decode.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • renderRawWithOfficial16() 会通过 JoRaw 类调用 joraw.js 或 joraw.wasm，让这套运行时执行 16 位解码。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • It configures demosaic quality, optional custom black levels, exposure behavior, and a custom color-matrix path if the user provided an invertible customMatrix.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 它会配置 demosaic 质量、可选的自定义黑电平、曝光行为，以及在用户提供可逆 customMatrix 时启用的自定义颜色矩阵路径。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • In the normal case, JoRaw can output camera-WB-driven sRGB-like RGB directly.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 在普通情况下，JoRaw 可以直接输出基于相机白平衡的类似 sRGB 的 RGB 结果。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • In the custom-matrix case, the function instead requests a camera-space style output, multiplies it by a derived matrix chain, applies exposure scaling, and then applies sRGB gamma manually.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 而在自定义矩阵情况下，这个函数会改为请求更接近相机空间的输出，然后自己乘上推导出的矩阵链、应用曝光缩放，并手动施加 sRGB gamma。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • This path is the reason standard mode can preserve a LibRaw-like look that is intentionally distinct from the project’s custom renderers.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 正是这条路径，让 standard 模式能够保留一种接近 LibRaw 的外观风格，并与项目自定义渲染器保持有意区分。

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              76. Zero-Dependency Render Families

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              76. Zero-Dependency 渲染家族

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • renderZeroDependencyFromRawImage() is the simplest path: it interprets each mosaic site independently, subtracts black level, applies exposure and simple Bayer-position white balance, and displays the result as grayscale.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • renderZeroDependencyFromRawImage() 是最简单的路径：它逐个解释马赛克采样点，减去黑电平，应用曝光和基于 Bayer 位置的简单白平衡，然后把结果以灰度形式显示出来。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • renderBasicColorZeroDepFromRawImage() is the project’s own full-color path, including normalization, white balance, optional RGGB or BGGR interpretation, a bilinear or AMAZE-like demosaic choice, and a camera-to-sRGB matrix stage.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • renderBasicColorZeroDepFromRawImage() 是项目自带的全彩路径，包含归一化、白平衡、可选的 RGGB 或 BGGR 解释、双线性或类 AMAZE 的 demosaic 选择，以及相机到 sRGB 的矩阵阶段。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • renderAdvancedZeroDepFromRawImage() is the scalar-unmix path, which uses user-provided bl, bg, and fg references to remap each mosaic sample into a common reference brightness domain.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • renderAdvancedZeroDepFromRawImage() 是 scalar-unmix 路径，它利用用户提供的 bl、bg 和 fg 参考，把每个马赛克采样重映射到统一参考亮度域中。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • That advanced mode does not just change color; it changes the signal interpretation itself by computing an alpha between background and foreground references and then extrapolating outside the calibrated range when needed.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 这种 advanced 模式并不只是改颜色，而是会改变信号解释本身：它通过计算背景与前景参考之间的 alpha，并在需要时对标定范围外进行外推。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • This is why the same RAW file can look radically different between standard, zero-dependency, zero-dep-color, and advanced-zero-dep.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 这也就是为什么同一张 RAW 在 standard、zero-dependency、zero-dep-color 和 advanced-zero-dep 之间会呈现出非常不同的结果。

Export-Oriented Render Paths

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              77. 面向导出的渲染路径

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Export behavior has changed materially: Ctrl+S is now a quick-save path for the current rendered result and exits through JDNG rather than only invoking the older generic save-modal flow.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 导出行为现在已经有了实质变化：Ctrl+S 会把当前渲染结果走一条 JDNG 快速保存路径，而不是只唤起旧的通用保存弹窗流程。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • For scalar or mosaic-style states, the quick-save path writes a single-plane JDNG and preserves CFA index or pattern information so later raw-domain work can still recover phase semantics.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 对于标量或马赛克风格状态，这条快速保存路径会写出单平面 JDNG，并保留 CFA 索引 / pattern 信息，以便后续 RAW 域工作仍能恢复相位语义。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • For full-color or true three-plane states, the quick-save path writes a three-plane JDNG instead of collapsing the view back into a single-plane mosaic container.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 对于全彩或真正三平面状态，这条快速保存路径会写出三平面 JDNG，而不会再把当前画面硬压回单平面马赛克容器。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The goal of this path is to preserve the current rendered result in a linear, non-stretched form that matches the visible render semantics more closely than the older export split did.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 这条路径的目标，是以线性、未拉伸的形式保留“当前界面渲染结果”，让导出语义比旧导出分叉更贴近屏幕上正在看的那种渲染含义。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • JDNG import logic is paired with that export design: single-plane JDNG reopens as an unrendered raw-mosaic style session, while native three-plane JDNG reopens as three-plane data.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 与之配套的 JDNG 导入逻辑也已经同步：单平面 JDNG 重新打开时会进入未渲染的 raw mosaic 风格会话，而原生三平面 JDNG 则会保持三平面状态。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Traditional DNG or TIFF service helpers still exist for other workflows, but they are no longer the best mental model for understanding what Ctrl+S does today.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 传统 DNG / TIFF 服务函数仍然服务于其他工作流，但它们已经不再是理解当下 Ctrl+S 行为的最佳心智模型。

How To Choose Among Render Modes

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              78. 如何在几种渲染模式之间做选择

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Choose standard when you want the closest thing this app has to an official RAW developer look and do not mind depending on the JoRaw runtime.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 当你想要最接近“官方 RAW 显影器观感”的结果，并且不介意依赖 JoRaw 运行时时，应选择 standard。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Choose grayscale zero-dependency when you want raw-site brightness inspection without committing to full demosaic color interpretation.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 当你想观察 RAW 采样点亮度，而不想过早进入全彩 demosaic 解释时，应选择灰度 zero-dependency。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Choose zero-dep-color when you want a full-color result but still want the project’s own demosaic and color pipeline instead of the JoRaw output look.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 当你想得到全彩图像，但又希望使用项目自己的 demosaic 和颜色管线，而不是 JoRaw 的输出风格时，应选择 zero-dep-color。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Choose advanced-zero-dep when the whole task is built around scalar unmixing, foreground and background references, or measurements that need that remapped signal domain.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 当整个任务围绕 scalar unmixing、前景/背景参考，或者需要这种重映射信号域的测量展开时，就应选择 advanced-zero-dep。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • In other words, render mode here is not a cosmetic preset; it changes the mathematical story of what the pixels mean.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 换句话说，这里的 render mode 不是视觉预设，而是在改变像素背后的数学含义。

Using DNG Export For Black-Level Fine Tuning

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              79. 用 DNG Export 做黑电平微调

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • DngExportModal.tsx is one of the most direct places in the app for black-level micro-adjustment because it lets you write a fresh 16-bit DNG container with explicit manual black levels.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • DngExportModal.tsx 是整个应用里最直接的黑电平微调入口之一，因为它允许你用显式手动黑电平重新写出一个新的 16 位 DNG 容器。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The 16bit Black Level (Manual Fill) field accepts either one number or four numbers.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 16bit Black Level (Manual Fill) 这个输入框既可以接受一个数，也可以接受四个数。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • If you enter one number, the code expands it to four identical values; if you enter fewer than four values, the code pads the rest with zero.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 如果你只输入一个数，代码会把它扩展成四个相同值；如果你输入不足四个值，代码会把剩余位置补零。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • That means you can use it both for coarse global tuning and for per-channel tuning when one quadrant or channel family sits differently.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 这意味着你既可以用它做粗略的全局微调，也可以在某个象限或某类通道位置偏移不一致时做分通道微调。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • In the Python DNG packer, these values are written into the BlackLevel tag together with BlackLevelRepeatDim = [2, 2], so they become metadata-level black offsets rather than just display-side offsets.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 在 Python DNG 打包器里，这些值会和 BlackLevelRepeatDim = [2, 2] 一起被写入 BlackLevel 标签，因此它们是元数据层面的黑电平偏置，而不仅仅是显示层偏置。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • The practical workflow is to export several nearby DNG variants, re-open them, and compare dark ROI means, shadow neutrality, and clipping headroom until the result stabilizes.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 最实用的流程是连续导出几份相邻数值的 DNG 变体，再重新载入比较暗场 ROI 均值、阴影中性度和高光余量，直到结果稳定为止。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • If the problem is mostly global pedestal, change all four numbers together.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 如果问题主要是全局 pedestal 偏移，就四个数一起改。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • If the problem looks like Bayer-position imbalance, change the four values independently and observe whether one color cast or checkerboard asymmetry disappears.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 如果问题更像 Bayer 位置不平衡，就把四个值拆开独立调整，并观察某种色偏或棋盘状不对称是否消失。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Bit Shift and Offset (+) are separate from black-level tuning and should not be confused with it.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Bit Shift 和 Offset (+) 与黑电平微调是两回事，不应混为一谈。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Bit Shift scales the raw code range upward into 16-bit container space, while Offset (+) adds a literal constant after that shift.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • Bit Shift 的作用是把原始码值范围放大到 16 位容器尺度，而 Offset (+) 则是在位移之后再直接加一个常数。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • In other words, Black Level changes the DNG metadata meaning, but Offset (+) changes the stored pixel values themselves.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 换句话说，Black Level 改的是 DNG 元数据层的解释，而 Offset (+) 改的是实际写入的像素值本身。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • For black-level micro-tuning, prefer changing Black Level first, and only use Offset (+) when you intentionally want to move the entire stored signal up or down.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 在做黑电平微调时，应优先调整 Black Level；只有在你明确想把整张图的实际存储信号整体抬高或压低时，才去用 Offset (+)。
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • A good verification habit is to reopen the exported DNG, inspect EXIF-like black-level interpretation externally if needed, and then measure central dark ROIs again in this app.
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              • 一个很好的验证习惯是把导出的 DNG 重新打开，必要时去外部软件检查黑电平解释，然后再回到本应用重新测量中央暗场 ROI。
